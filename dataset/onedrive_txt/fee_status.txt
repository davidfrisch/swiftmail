Fee Status

You can find information about student fee status in this link: https://www.ucl.ac.uk/students/fees-and-funding/pay-your-fees/fee-schedules/student-fee-status 

  

During your application process, you will be asked to upload supporting documents including your passport so that UCL can verify your fee status. The UCL Graduate Admissions is responsible for making the assessment of your fee status and the assessment is communicated via UCL's formal offer of admission. If you have further questions about your fee status, please contact Graduate Admissions via their website: Contact Graduate and Teacher Training Admissions 
https://www.ucl.ac.uk/prospective-students/graduate/admissions-enquiries


We (the academic department) is not authorised to assess student fee status.  
 
If you have further questions about applying for an MSc programme at the Computer Science department, you can contact us at cs.pgt-admissions@ucl.ac.uk. Please include your full name, student ID number (if applicable), and the programme you are applying for/interested in studying.
